About Us

At Debris Watch, we focus on tracking real-time space debris to help users monitor orbital hazards with precision. Our platform delivers up-to-date information on debris locations, providing a reliable tool for understanding and addressing the growing challenge of space debris in Earth's orbit.

Our Solution: The DebriSnare

DebriSnare are advanced robots built to collect up to 150 kg of space debris and return it to Earth. Constructed from sustainable materials, they self-decompose if they malfunction, preventing further debris accumulation. Their design promotes a cleaner orbital environment.
